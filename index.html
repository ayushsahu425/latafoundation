About Us Empowering communities through compassion, education, and sustainable development.

Smt. Lata Education and Welfare Trust, founded on the 24th of July, 2006, is a shining example of unwavering commitment to positive change. Every year, our health center extends its healing touch to hundreds, addressing their medical needs and fostering well-being. Simultaneously, our technical center equips dozens of individuals with the skills and knowledge necessary to empower themselves and find gainful employment, promoting selfsufficiency.

FREE EDUCATION INITIATIVE

Through our collaboration with Kala Vidhya Mandir, the Trust provides free education to children from economically disadvantaged families. This initiative ensures that children who face financial and social barriers are given equal access to quality education in a supportive and encouraging environment. By removing the burden of educational expenses, we help families prioritize their children's learning and future development.

About The Founder Mrs. Lata Chavan

Mrs. Lata Chavan, the esteemed founder of Smt. Lata Education and Welfare Trust, is an inspiring visionary and a dedicated philanthropist with a profound commitment to social betterment. Her unwavering passion for creating positive change in the community and her relentless efforts have been the driving force behind the trust's mission and numerous impactful initiatives.

Mrs. Lata Chavan's deep empathy for the underprivileged, the marginalized, and the environment has led to the establishment of a wide array of projects, touching the lives of countless individuals. Her compassionate leadership and unwavering dedication have enabled the trust to provide crucial healthcare services, educational opportunities, and sustainable environmental solutions to those in need.

With her compassionate heart and visionary spirit, Mrs. Lata Chavan continues to be a beacon of hope and a source of inspiration for the trust's team, beneficiaries, and the broader community. Her tireless work and commitment to making the world a better place through education, healthcare, and environmental conservation stand as a testament to the positive impact that one person's vision and determination can have on the lives of many.
