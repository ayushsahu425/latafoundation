Learn More About Us Our Commitment & Vision

Smt. Lata Education and Welfare Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged and marginalized communities through compassion and service. We work tirelessly to provide opportunities that enable individuals to live with dignity and self-reliance.

Get Involved
Our Philosophy
We believe education is the most powerful tool for transformation and that investing in the future of children is investing in the future of society. Our work is guided by the belief that even small acts of service can create powerful waves of positive change.
Our Mission
To empower individuals and communities through inclusive and sustainable initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and social welfare. We strive to provide access to quality learning and essential healthcare for vulnerable communities.
Core Values
Our organization is built on the pillars of Compassion, Empowerment, Sustainability, Inclusivity, Integrity, Collaboration, and Innovation.
