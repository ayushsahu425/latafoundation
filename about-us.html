About Us Empowering Communities Through Compassion & Service

Established on 24 July 2006 , Smt. Lata Education and Welfare Trust (Lata Foundation) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged and marginalized communities. Guided by a strong vision of social welfare and inclusive growth, the Lata Foundation and Welfare trust works tirelessly to provide opportunities that enable individuals to live with dignity, hope, and self-reliance.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through inclusive and sustainable initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and social welfare. Smt. Lata Education and Welfare Trust strives to provide access to quality education for underprivileged children, essential healthcare services for vulnerable communities, and skill development opportunities that promote self-reliance and economic independence.

To create a society where every individual has the opportunity to live with dignity, access essential resources, and realize their full potential. We envision empowered communities where education, healthcare, and sustainable development work together to build a better future, while the environment is protected and preserved for generations to come.

Voices of Vision

Dear Friends, Supporters, and Well-Wishers, It is with immense pride, humility, and gratitude that I present this Annual Report of Smt. Lata Education and Welfare Trust. Since the establishment of the Trust on 24 July 2006, our journey has been driven by a deep commitment to serve humanity and uplift those who are most in need. What began as a heartfelt vision to support underprivileged communities has today grown into a collective effort that touches the lives of many individuals and families. Each year, our work reminds us that even small acts of compassion and service can create powerful waves of positive change in society.

Over the years, Smt. Lata Education and Welfare Trust has worked tirelessly in the areas of education, healthcare, community welfare, women's empowerment, and environmental sustainability. Our mission has always been to ensure that individuals from marginalized and economically disadvantaged backgrounds receive opportunities that enable them to lead dignified and meaningful lives. Through our educational initiatives, we strive to provide children with access to quality learning and a supportive environment where they can grow academically and personally. We strongly believe that education is the most powerful tool for transformation and that investing in the future of children is investing in the future of society.

Mrs. Lata Chavan
Founder

Over the years, our organization has remained committed to serving the community through meaningful initiatives in education, healthcare, social welfare, and environmental awareness. Each step of this journey has been guided by our shared vision of building a society where every individual has the opportunity to live with dignity and hope.

Your continued encouragement strengthens our resolve to serve the community with sincerity and compassion. Together, we are making a meaningful difference in the lives of many people.

Anil Chavan
Chairperson
